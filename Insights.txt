Insights from E-commerce Sales Analysis

1. The Electronics category generates the highest average revenue (65,440.42), indicating strong demand and high-value transactions.

2. Among sub-categories, Laptops contribute the highest average revenue (66,781.09), followed by Clothing.

3. Furniture is the most sold category with 5,247 units, followed by Footwear (4,962 units). However, Laptops generate the highest revenue despite lower sales volume (3,439 units), indicating a high price per unit.

4. Megan Johnson is the highest revenue-generating customer, followed by John Smith and Thomas Johnson, showing that a small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue.

5. North Carolina is the top-performing state in terms of revenue, followed by Arizona and Oklahoma, highlighting key regional markets.

6. Orders are delivered very efficiently, with an average delivery time of approximately 1 day, reflecting strong operational performance.

7. Payment methods are evenly distributed: Cash on Delivery (25.41%), Card (25.25%), Net Banking (24.90%), and UPI (24.44%), indicating no strong dependency on a single payment mode.

8. The brand "Wright, Miller and Maddox" generates the highest revenue, showing strong brand performance.

9. Discounts reduce revenue by an average of 11,293.60, indicating a measurable impact of discounting on overall sales.
